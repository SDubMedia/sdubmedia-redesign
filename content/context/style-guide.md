# SDub Media — Style Guide

## Formatting Rules
- Short paragraphs — 2-4 sentences
- Vary sentence length — not every sentence the same rhythm
- No bullet lists with exactly four items every single time
- Headers should be questions or statements, not generic labels
- Bold the most important phrase in a section, not everything

## Words to Avoid
leverage, utilize, delve, game-changing, unlock, elevate, cutting-edge,
innovative, seamlessly, robust, scalable, bespoke, synergy, deep dive,
at the end of the day, it's worth noting, in conclusion

## Structure for Blog Posts
1. Hook — start with a real situation or a direct statement
2. The problem or question
3. The answer (what we actually do / what works)
4. Real Nashville examples where possible
5. Call to action — low pressure, one line

## Images
- Every post needs a header image (AI generated is fine)
- Images should feel cinematic — dark tones, real-world Nashville settings
- No stock-photo energy

## SEO Notes
- Target one primary keyword per post
- Use it naturally in the title, first paragraph, and one subheader
- Don't stuff it
