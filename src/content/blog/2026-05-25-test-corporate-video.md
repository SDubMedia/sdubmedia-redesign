Most businesses think about corporate video the same way they think about a new logo — a one-time project with a clear finish line. That framing is where most of the budget gets wasted.

## What corporate video actually means

When a Nashville business says they need "a corporate video," they usually mean one of five different things. A brand story for the homepage. A recruiting video. A product explainer. Event coverage. Or a recurring content series.

Each one has a different budget, a different crew, and a different timeline.

## What it costs

Here is the honest range for Nashville in 2026:

- A single brand story video: $3,000 to $8,000
- A half-day of event coverage: $1,500 to $3,000
- A monthly content retainer: $2,500 to $6,000 per month

The number that matters is not the day rate. It is the cost per usable asset.

## Is it worth it?

A well-made brand video keeps working for two to three years. The cost per impression drops every month it stays live.
