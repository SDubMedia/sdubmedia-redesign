Let's Work Together

Recurring video for businesses. A few weddings a year. Tell us what you're planning.

We're SDub Media, based in Nashville. We work with businesses on recurring content — weekly video, podcasts, agent reels, branded events, and headshot days — and we take a limited number of weddings and portrait sessions each year.

For business inquiries

Tell us what you need — whether it's a one-off shoot or something ongoing. If you want recurring content, mention how often and what kind.

For weddings

Send us your date and venue so we can check availability. We book a small number of weddings each year and dates go fast.

For portrait or family sessions

Let us know the session type, preferred date, and location.

Response time

We reply within 24 hours on weekdays. Weekends we're usually behind a camera — we'll catch up Monday.
