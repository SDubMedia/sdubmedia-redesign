About

Who We Are

A Nashville production team built around one thing: showing up, every week, with content that actually ships.

Meet Geoff

I'm Geoff Southworth — founder, lead videographer, and the person behind the camera on almost every shoot. I started SDub Media because I kept seeing the same problem: businesses that needed consistent content but couldn't find a production partner who actually delivered on schedule. So I built a team around solving that.

Most weeks you'll find us somewhere in middle Tennessee — a Coldwell Banker Southern Realty office shooting the week's agent videos, a podcast studio capturing three episodes in a day, an awards ceremony, a headshot session, a recruitment shoot. We keep a busy commercial calendar because consistency is what businesses actually need. Not one great video. Fifty of them, every year, on time.

We also take a small number of weddings each year — eight, intentionally capped. Every couple gets us for the entire day, arrival through the last dance. If you're planning one, the pitch is simple: we've been doing this long enough to disappear when you need us to and step in when you don't.

The Team

SDub Media isn't a solo act. We're a small, trusted crew that scales to match the day — and every person behind the camera has been hand-picked for consistency, reliability, and craft.
