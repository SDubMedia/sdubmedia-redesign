## Their Strategy (how they built 1,457 keyword rankings)

Tone Films operates as a **multi-city video production company** with individual location pages
for every market. Their playbook:

1. Build a city-specific landing page per market
2. Each page targets "[city] video production" and ranks for "near me" searches
3. When someone in that city searches "video production near me," Google serves the local page

**Their known location pages:**
- `/dallas-video-production/` — primary traffic driver
- `/fort-worth-video-production/`
- `/plano-video-production/`
- `/nashville-video-production/`
- `/dc-video-production/`
- `/nyc-video-production/`
- `/OKC-video-production/`
- `/Los-Angeles-video-production/`

## What They Do Well

1. **City page blueprint** — simple template, replicated across 8+ markets. Works for "near me."
2. **Multi-city authority** — being in Dallas, DC, Nashville, NYC etc. makes them look like a national brand.
3. **Early mover** — they got in before most competitors built dedicated city pages.
4. **Breadth over depth** — they rank for a lot of things at positions 3-7, not a few things at #1.

---

## Where There's a Gap

**They own no Nashville niche content.** Their Nashville page covers "video production" generically.
They have NOTHING specific to:
- Nashville corporate video / brand video
- Nashville real estate video
- Nashville podcast production
- Nashville event videographer
- Nashville headshot / recap content
- Industry-specific Nashville content (real estate teams, schools, nonprofits)

**Their Nashville positions are weak (3-6).** A site that actually focuses on Nashville specifically
— not a generic multi-city page — should be able to rank above them for Nashville terms.

**Their traffic is declining.** Down 12-13% in the past month. Something changed — possibly
Google update, or they stopped publishing content. Either way, there's a window right now.

**"Near me" is wide open.** Their KD on near-me terms is 13-19. Absurdly low. These are
the terms driving their biggest volume. SDub Media needs a strong Nashville-optimized homepage
and service pages to capture local "near me" searches.
