# Real Estate Photo Teaching Cluster — Keyword Research
Source: SEMrush Keyword Magic Tool screenshots, 2026-05-25 (national/US, informational intent).
Goal: SEO traffic. Secondary: soft upsell to real estate brand video. All downloads FREE (no gates).

## Headline insight
GEAR posts are the traffic engine here — high volume, near-zero competition. The technique
topics (lighting, composition, prep, shot list) have almost no direct search demand and belong
as SECTIONS inside the pillar, not standalone posts. This whole space is low-volume overall
(winnable but modest traffic), so it's an authority play, not a flood.

## Low / no demand → fold into the PILLAR as sections (not standalone)
- real estate photo lighting — ~0 volume
- real estate photo shot list — no data (use "checklist" instead)
- common real estate photography mistakes — 20 (anchor with the rule #1 lens-clean tip)
- how to photograph a house — noisy/junk (white house photographer, butterfly house)
- composition, room prep — no meaningful direct keyword

## Wrong intent → skip
- real estate photography jobs / career / classes / salary (job seekers, students)
- "find old real estate listing photos", "removed", "reused" (people hunting old photos)
- pricing/packages/rates/services/companies (commercial — that's the hire-a-pro intent, not DIY)
- all city names (denver/dallas/houston/austin) — local commercial, not our national teaching play
