How we shoot weddings

One team. The whole day. Yours.

Every wedding is different, so we keep packages simple, flexible, and built around what matters most to you. When you hire SDub Media, you're hiring us for the entire day — we arrive before you do and we leave when you leave — so you never feel rushed or limited by the clock.

Couples often tell us we're not just their videographers — we're their personal hype team. Guiding when you need it, blending in when you don't, stepping in to help with whatever comes up so you can stay present. We bring fun, natural direction for photos and video without turning your wedding into a production.
